# Outreach Templates

Universal B2B templates for cold email, LinkedIn, and follow-ups.

---

## Cold Email Templates

### Template 1: The Observation + Curiosity

**Subject**: Quick question about [specific observation]

Hey [Name],

Noticed [specific observation about their company/role].

Curious - are you [experiencing problem this suggests]?

Either way, thought it was worth reaching out.

[Your name]

---

### Template 2: The Trigger Event

**Subject**: Congrats on [trigger event]

Hey [Name],

Saw the news about [funding, new hire, expansion].

In my experience, that usually means [consequence that creates need].

If that's on your radar, happy to share what's working. If not, no worries.

[Your name]

---

### Template 3: The Insight Lead

**Subject**: [Counterintuitive insight]

Hey [Name],

Most [their role] I talk to are [common approach].

Turns out that's actually causing [negative consequence].

The companies getting better results are [alternative approach].

Worth a 10-minute call to see if this applies?

[Your name]

---

### Template 4: The Direct Ask

**Subject**: Quick ask

Hey [Name],

I help [target persona] with [problem].

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to see if it's relevant?

If not, no worries at all.

[Your name]

---

### Template 5: The Problem Agitation

**Subject**: [Specific problem] still happening?

Hey [Name],

Is [specific problem] still a thing at [Company]?

Most teams I talk to deal with [symptom 1], [symptom 2], and [symptom 3].

If that sounds familiar, I've got ideas. If not, feel free to ignore.

[Your name]

---

## LinkedIn Templates

### Connection Request (under 300 chars)

**Option A - Common ground**:
Hey [Name], saw we're both [common ground]. Would love to connect.

**Option B - Content appreciation**:
Hey [Name], your post on [topic] resonated. Would love to connect.

**Option C - Direct**:
Hey [Name], I work with [their role] on [problem]. Not pitching - just thought we should connect.

---

### Post-Connection Message

Wait 1-2 days, then:

Hey [Name], thanks for connecting.

Quick question - is [problem] something you're actively working on, or more back-burner?

No pitch. Just curious.
